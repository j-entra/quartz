# Digital Garden

## Hotkeys
1. open quick switcher (cmd-o)
2. search (cmd-shift-f)
3. back (opt-a) 
4. new note (cmd-n)
5. open note in new window (cmd-click on note)
6. new pane (opt-cmd)
7. move between words (opt-left/right)
8. move between paragraphs (opt-up/down)
